RGB Lightsaber Project Roadmap:
RGBSaber Control Board:
1) RGBSaber_Control v1.6  (Standardized 0805 SMD parts, SMD Bulk Capacitor, Improved DC converter, Improved silkscreening)
2) RGBSaber_Control v2.0  (Improved connectors, Modular Daughterboard System)

RGBSaber Sound Driver:
1) RGBSaber_Sound v1.0	  (Basic Operational Model, MicroSD card support)

RGBSaber Transistor Driver Boards:
1) RGBSaber_3W v1.0	   (Basic Operational Model)
2) RGBSaber_5W v1.0	   (Basic Operational Model)
3) RGBSaber_10W v1.0   (Basic Operational Model)

RGBSaber Hilt Organization System:
1) RGBSaber_Hilt v1.0  (Basic Operational Model)

RGBSaber Firmware:
1) Firmware v2.0 (C-code rewrite, digital indicator light dimming, improved control scheme)
2) Firmware v3.0 (SoundFX, FlashFX, user-selectable/saveable colors, color quickselect, improved sleep mode)
3) Firmware v4.0 (SDCard Wear Protection)
